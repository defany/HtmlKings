One piece

The epic tale of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew sailing across dangerous seas in search of the ultimate treasure. With rubber-like powers from eating a Devil Fruit, Luffy battles powerful foes while making unforgettable friends on his journey to become King of the Pirates.

Jujutsu Kaisen

High school student Yuji Itadori swallows a cursed finger to save friends, becoming host to the powerful curse Sukuna. He enters the dangerous world of Jujutsu Sorcerers who battle curses while training to control the deadly power within him.

Attack on Titan

To combat the man-eating Titans that have forced humanity behind massive walls, young Eren Yeager joins the military. After his home is destroyed, he fights to reclaim the world from the giants in a brutal and desperate war for survival.

Dandadan

A quirky supernatural adventure where a high school girl who believes in ghosts teams up with a boy who believes in aliens. Together they encounter bizarre spirits, aliens, and cosmic mysteries while navigating their awkward teenage relationship.
