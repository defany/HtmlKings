Who Am I?

Hey! I'm an anime enthusiast and web designer currently living in Japan. I created Anime Heaven as a cozy corner for everyone who shares a love for anime art, stories, and culture. My goal is to bring together fans from around the world and celebrate the creativity and emotion that make anime so special. When I’m not coding or designing, I’m usually watching the latest seasonal shows, sketching fan art, or exploring Tokyo’s anime shops.

The Site Idea

The idea behind Anime Heaven was simple — to create a place that feels like home for anime fans. I wanted a space where people could discover beautiful art, share their favorite moments, and talk about the stories that inspire us. This site is built with love for both design and anime — combining clean visuals, smooth animations, and a community spirit that captures the heart of Japanese pop culture.

My Favorite Anime

One Piece

“One Piece” has been my all-time favorite since I was a kid. It’s not just about pirates and treasure — it’s about freedom, friendship, and chasing your dreams no matter how far they are. Luffy’s endless optimism and the Straw Hat crew’s bond always remind me why I fell in love with anime in the first place. Every episode feels like an adventure, and the emotions hit just as strong even after hundreds of episodes.
